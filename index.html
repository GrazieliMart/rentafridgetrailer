About Us

Rentafridgetrailer was established in 2018 as there was a shortage of affordable refrigerated/freezer trailers for hire , we have expanded our client base to most areas of Ireland.

Our friendly, helpful and dedicated team can deliver to any location at any time, day or night. We provide one of the most professional and reliable services for refrigeration refits, temperature controlled storage and on-site backup when you need it most. We are located in North County Wicklow. For further enquiries please get in touch via the contact page or give us a call.
